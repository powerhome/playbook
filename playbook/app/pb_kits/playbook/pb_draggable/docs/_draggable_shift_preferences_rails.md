This example mirrors the Sales Schedule Shift Preferences Rails layout: two side-by-side ranked lists of day cards with grip handles, toggles, and a save action.

Use a mobile viewport or device simulator to try touch drag. Grab the grip on a card to reorder within a column; swipe the card body to scroll the page.

Each column uses its own `draggable` kit with a line drop zone. Cards use `draggable_item`, `drag_handle`, and a primary side highlight to match the product mockup.
